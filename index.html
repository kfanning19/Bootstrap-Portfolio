About Me


After a childhood spent buried in books and various arts and crafts, I fell in love with ancient languages in college- yes, I am used to being looked at like I'm insane when I say Latin is fun and yes, my brain does try to read lorem ipsum. To pay for the large number of books a Latin major required, I started supplementing my income by selling knitting patterns on several websites.

Looking to get back into a job that is more satisfying, several people said coding would be a good fit. So I signed up for a bootcamp, and here I am.
